How I survived coding bootcamp

November, 3, 2022

Completing a coding bootcamp is a challenging and rewarding experience. In this blog post, I will share some tips on how I survived my coding bootcamp and came out the other side a stronger and more skilled developer. One key thing that helped me survive my coding bootcamp was staying organized and focused. I used a planner to keep track of my assignments and deadlines, and I made sure to prioritize my work so that I was always working on the most important tasks first. This helped me stay on top of my workload and avoid getting overwhelmed.

Another thing that helped me survive my coding bootcamp was staying positive and motivated. There were times when the material was challenging and I wanted to give up, but I reminded myself of why I was doing the bootcamp and the goals that I wanted to achieve. This helped me push through the tough times and keep moving forward. In addition to staying organized and motivated, I also made sure to take care of myself during my coding bootcamp. This included getting enough sleep, eating well, and taking regular breaks to recharge. By taking care of myself, I was able to stay focused and perform at my best during the bootcamp.

Overall, surviving a coding bootcamp requires a combination of organization, motivation, and self-care. By following these tips, I was able to overcome the challenges of the bootcamp and come out the other side a stronger and more skilled developer.
